Welcome to Mallet
Stratis collection is one among our collections that is crafted with utmost dedication and care. The main focus of Lilo collection is to ensure maximum comfort to its users and high functionality. Hence, the design of every piece makes it a reality. Every piece of furniture has been made considering ergonomics in its design elements which ensures the satisfaction of its users.

The Stratis

Stratis is very distinctive in its design with the wood bending at perfect proportions making it look very fluid. The lounger is made to look fluidic by fusing the back, seat, and legs in wood. Whereas the hind leg and hand rest are made of metal.


The Air Shelf

The main challenge with this piece was translating the abstract artwork of Pier Mondrian from the early 20th century to a contemporary 3-dimensional furniture piece fashioned out of wood. Much thought was devoted to the exact arrangement of the various elements, to achieve the modular yet perfectly unified aesthetic of Mondrianâ€™s work. In terms of craftsmanship, the process involved creating separate panels and adding in fixture details to complete the overall form and aesthetic of the shelf.


The Scoot

To create the Scoot, four planks were aligned such that their grains met at the center of the seat. Metal plates were used to highlight the tapers and also create the illusion of the missing edges.

The Cornula

The combination of flowing curves and emphatic lines makes the Cornula stand out in any setting. The combination of wood and metal highlights the contrast in form while creating a solid stable structure.
