The Obtuse

Story

This side stand has a simple, solid design that evokes the square. The sharp features and angles of the piece accentuate the simplicity of the lines and form. As the name indicates, Obtuse highlights a single angle mirrored on both sides, presenting a beautiful symmetry, while also providing stability.

Design

One of the main challenges in taking this piece from concept to form was in creating the angle of the vertical elements, which adds a layer of complexity to the joinery holding up the entire structure.

The Pick

Story

A beechwood tabletop with an exaggerated grain pattern tapered oak wood legs and developed truncated cone metal bracket. The Pick table is an integral part of the Lucerna Collection, with its aesthetically pleasing minimal design, and simple but effective structural solutions in a geometrically sound form.

Design

We achieved the geometry of the tabletop with set proportions and radius in a calculated ratio, such that every curve complements each other. The process of creating this piece involved the use of innovative jigs to taper the legs and applied geometry to achieve the perfect proportions with the metal brackets.
