Welcome to Mallet
The masterpiece of the collection, it comprises four solid columns that resemble beautifully layered ancient tree trunks that form the base for a perfectly rectangular flat tabletop. The complex patterns and grains of each column are balanced by the simple lines of the tabletop and overall shape of the table. This unorthodox piece will definitely be a talking point in any shared space, with its solidity and assertiveness.

The Lilo

The Lilo stands out for its unique design. Apart from the panel cladding on the back, the handrail is made up of four blocks of wood parametrically cut and shaped to achieve the curve, hence making it the most beautiful part of the product.


The Stump Table

The Stump Table represents our interpretation of the organic form of a tree stump. A parametric digitally created shape forms the base of the unit, topped by a pure rectangular flat table surface. This piece is the quintessential example of Mallet’s creativity in furniture design, from conceptualization through to technique in execution and finish.


The Architecture

This vintage-looking shelf has a bold design that highlights the element of stability in its form. The base is made thick with broad pieces of wood to ensure strength. The most important thing you would generally lookout for in a shelf would be the storage space and we are sure you wouldn’t get disappointed in that regard.


The Insula

Insula consists of a red oak band running through the console which acts as a support for the structure, and also as handles for the storage drawers. Insula comprises simple curves and chamfers which create a holistic experience.


The Queven

The real beauty of Queven lies in its construction technique. This cantilevered backrest chair is made of three layers of solid red oak. The unique design makes the appearance classy and extraordinary.
