The Lilo

Story

The Lilo collection emphasizes comfort, a great example of this being the Lilo single seater sofa. The curves created by the neatly arranged pieces of wood talks a great deal about the inclusion of contemporary design element in its form.

Design

The Lilo stands out for its unique design. Apart from the panel cladding on the back, the handrail is made up of four blocks of wood parametrically cut and shaped to achieve the curve, hence making it the most beautiful part of the product.

The Architecture

Story

Architecture is an ode to neoclassical furniture with modern construction methods. The form embraces bold and straight lines as pure geometric shapes. It is a mid-size shelving unit that has good storage space. Timeless design elements can be noted on the cornices, threshold, proportions, and geometry.

Design

This vintage-looking shelf has a bold design that highlights the element of stability in its form. The base is made thick with broad pieces of wood to ensure strength. The most important thing you would generally lookout for in a shelf would be the storage space and we are sure you wouldn’t get disappointed in that regard.

The Insula

Story

This very consolidated design piece revolves around its functionality and virtue. The Insula is a rare TV console that catches your attention owing to its exquisite design. An important feature of the Insula is that it comes with ample storage space required to keep all electronic accessories. Insula could be that functional piece of art in your drawing room!

Design

Insula consists of a red oak band running through the console which acts as a support for the structure, and also as handles for the storage drawers. Insula comprises simple curves and chamfers which create a holistic experience.

The Queven

Story

Queven is the perfect combination of sophistication and grace. The design of the chair is exceptional with the smooth curves connecting the arm support and the backrest. It is a single-seater made with red oak wood which enhances the brightness of the form, making Queven even more desirable.

Design

The real beauty of Queven lies in its construction technique. This cantilevered backrest chair is made of three layers of solid red oak. The unique design makes the appearance classy and extraordinary.
